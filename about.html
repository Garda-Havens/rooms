About the Property

Garda Havens offers a tranquil escape just moments away from Gardaland and Lake Garda. Nestled in a serene countryside setting, our newly updated rooms, which accommodate up to 6 guests, are perfect for families or anyone seeking a peaceful retreat from city life. Enjoy comfort and relaxation in a lush, green environment, designed to enhance your stay.

About Attractions

Welcome to Peschiera del Garda! You’re perfectly positioned to explore a wealth of attractions. Start your adventure at Gardaland, Italy’s top amusement park, and next door, dive into the fun at Movieland Park, known for its exciting rides and shows. For a refreshing day out, Caneva Aquapark offers thrilling water slides and pools for all ages.

Take advantage of Lake Garda with scenic boat tours or dive into water sports like sailing and fishing. The area also features superb cycling and hiking trails that offer breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding landscape.

Explore the historic town of Sirmione, known for its ancient castle and thermal baths, or delve into the rich heritage of Verona, famous for its Roman amphitheater. The charming towns of Lazise and Bardolino offer scenic lakeside strolls and wine tastings. Enhance your experience with local markets, fresh produce and unique souvenirs!

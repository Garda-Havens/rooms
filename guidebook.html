Towns & Villages to Visit 10 destinations
Peschiera del Garda ~5 km
UNESCO fortress with navigable canals and starting point for the Mincio cycle route.
visitpeschiera.it
Lazise ~8.5 km
Italy's first free municipality – medieval walls, Scaligero castle and Venetian customs house.
visitlazise.it
Bardolino ~16.5 km
Capital of Bardolino and Chiaretto wine, with a lakeside promenade and wine museum.
visitbardolino.it
Garda (village) ~20 km
The village that gave the lake its name – panoramic Rocca, Palazzo dei Capitani and Punta San Vigilio.
visitgarda.com
Sirmione ~14 km
The "pearl of Lake Garda" – moated castle, Roman villa and thermal spas.
visitsirmione.com
Malcesine ~45 km
Medieval village with a Scaligero castle and cable car to Monte Baldo.
visitmalcesine.com
Borghetto sul Mincio ~15 km
Watermills and romantic houses on the Mincio river – famous for Valeggio tortellini.
valeggio.com
Desenzano del Garda ~20 km
The lake's largest city – harbour, Roman Villa with mosaics and nightlife.
visitdesenzano.it
Castellaro Lagusello ~16 km
Medieval village with a heart-shaped lake in the morainic hills, among Italy's most beautiful.

funiviedelbaldo.it

lessinia.verona.it
Rocca di Manerba ~35 km
Natural promontory on the Brescia shore with nature reserve and panoramic trails.
visitbrescia.it
Mincio cycling path ~5 km

parcodelmincio.it

Extensive collection of rare plants with works by Haring and Lichtenstein, at Gardone Riviera.
hellergarden.com

Culture, History & Monuments 8 sites
Visconti Tower & Castle – Castelnuovo ~2.5 km
Visconti tower of 14th-century origin at the heart of Castelnuovo's medieval walls.
tourism.verona.it
Grotte di Catullo – Sirmione ~19 km
The largest Roman villa in northern Italy (1st c. AD), set in an olive grove with lake views.
visitsirmione.com
Verona Arena ~23 km
Roman amphitheatre from the 1st c. AD – summer Opera Festival since 1913.
arena.it
Verona historic centre (UNESCO) ~23 km
Piazza Bra, Juliet's House, Castelvecchio, Scaligero Bridge. UNESCO World Heritage.
tourism.verona.it
Vittoriale degli Italiani – Gardone Riviera ~48 km
D'Annunzio's estate with house-museum, parks and the battleship Puglia embedded in the hillside.
vittoriale.it
Scaligero Castle – Malcesine ~49 km
One of Italy's best-preserved castles – museum and panoramic tower over the lake.
visitmalcesine.com
Sanctuary of Madonna della Corona ~33 km
Sanctuary carved into the cliff above the Adige – a dramatic walk up panoramic staircases.
madonnadellacorona.it
Tower of San Martino della Battaglia ~15 km
Risorgimento tower with ossuary and museum – views over the 1859 battlefield.
visitbrescia.it

Verona food tour ~25 km

Cantina Corte Fornello & pasta course ~11 km
